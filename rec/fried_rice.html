Fried Rice Recipe

decription

Homemade fried rice is a quick and flavorful dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, eggs, and seasonings. It's customizable, budget-friendly, and perfect for using leftover rice. The key to great fried rice is high heat, dry (day-old) rice, and simple seasoning that brings everything together.

ingredents

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably chilled overnight)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil (or sesame oil for extra flavor)
  • ½ cup frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • ¼ cup diced onion
  • 1-2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 eggs

step-by-step

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok on medium-high. Add 1 tbsp oil.
  2. Add eggs and scramble until just cooked.
  3. Add another tbsp of oil.
  4. Stir-fry onion for 1–2 minutes, then add garlic.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and cook until warm.
  6. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes, pressing down any clumps and letting the rice toast slightly.
  7. Mix well until everything is evenly coated.
  8. Stir in the scrambled eggs and any cooked meat or tofu.
  9. Mix for another 1–2 minutes.
  10. Add salt, pepper, and green onions. Taste and adjust seasonings. Serve hot!
